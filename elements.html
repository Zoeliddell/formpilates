FORM Instructor Training Courses

FORM has been at the forefront of delivering Dynamic Reformer Pilates in New Zealand for over a decade. Since opening the first flagship studio in Mount Maunganui, we have expanded our brand by opening various studios around New Zealand and developed this platform to share our knowledge with others.

We aim to provide an education and knowledge base for one to mold into their own method of practice, instead of formatting more rigid sequences and practices that must be delivered in a specific manner.

In-Studio Courses

Reform's Module 1, four day, in-studio training will start with orienting you to the reformer and associated equipment and end with you planning your own classes. The in-between consists of learning over 50 exercises with invaluable practical practice and feedback from two of our industry leading facilitators. You'll leave feeling educated, invigorated and potentially a little overwhelmed.

Online Courses

If you're unable to attend our in-studio training, then online is the option for you. All content is delivered through an in-depth online platform offering verbal cues, supporting videos and imagery and spring regressions and progressions. You can work through the modules at your own pace offering flexibility and a more time considerate option. Both Module 1 (if you're new to the reformer) and Module 2 (for those with reformer experience or who have completed Module 1) are available online.
